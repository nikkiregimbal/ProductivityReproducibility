\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}

This project assesses the effects of anthropogenic impacts on aquatic
vertebrates in Mack Creek, Oregon.

The data is available as the \textbf{and\_vertebrates} dataset from the
\emph{lterdatasampler} package in R.

This data assesses differences in biomass for 3 vertebrates compared
between a anthropogenically disturbed (previously clear cut) and
anthropogenically undisturbed (old growth) sites along the creek.

Previous research from (Kaylor and Warren 2017) indicated that forest
disturbances can affect vertebrate mass. This research inspired this
research project, specifically looking at Coastal Cutthroat Trout,
Coastal Giant Salamander and Cascade Torrent Salamander.
